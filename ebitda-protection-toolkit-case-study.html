Context

Three shocks, one repeatable system

Multiple macro and internal events required focused special projects to maintain profitability:

  • 2020 COVID disruption
  • 2023 Margin degrowth risk
  • 2025 EdTech slowdown from AI disruption

The business needed a rigorous, proactive intervention engine linking revenue scenarios, cost actions, and monthly performance tracking.

The Engine

A four-phase cycle

Used in 2020, 2023, and 2025 — not rebuilt each time, just re-run.

01 — Forecast & Simulate

Model the range

Monthly revenue and cost forecasting with vertical sales, operations, and SG&A leads. Worst-, base-, and best-case P&L simulations set actuals-plus-forecast against budget at both revenue and EBITDA level.

02 — Target & Assign

Name the gap

Target EBITDA fixed at base-case revenue. The shortfall split into specific cost and revenue actions, each with an owner and a monthly dollar value — collected within two weeks.

03 — Execute & Govern

Protect the investments worth keeping

Strategic investments reviewed through a Finance and CFO-led ROI lens before approval. HR partnered on named headcount action lists, termination timelines, and daily/weekly CXO trackers.

04 — Monitor & Carry Forward

Hold the gains

Actual profitability tracked monthly against target, with future-month interventions adjusted for new risk or opportunity. Structural gains held in place until the top line recovered. Carried forward structural gains into future-year P&L by maintaining controls until topline recovered.​
