Context

The challenge of immediate governance alignment

A high-growth enterprise specializing in data analytics and AI for global enterprises was acquired by a private equity firm, triggering immediate organizational needs:

  • The Acquisition Required rapid institutional integration into the PE firm's rigid reporting, operational governance, and financial review standards.
  • The Objective Establish unmatched pipeline transparency, executive reporting consistency, and absolute investor confidence from Day One.

The solution demanded an adaptive interim operational layer connecting corporate financial data directly to the private equity operating team and Board metrics.

Actions Taken

The implementation engine

Parallel execution tracks running over a 4-month transition timeline to engineer a unified reporting stack.

01 — Core infrastructure

Form dedicated IR team

Assembled a specialized 2-person Investor Relations team combining institutional FP&A models with rigorous M&A reporting frameworks to manage the workflow baseline.

02 — Strategic alignment

OGSM & target mapping

Coordinated the overarching 5-year OGSM strategy across multiple workstreams. Transformed complex consultant output and recommendations into actionable corporate goals within 2.5 months.

03 — Financial architecture

5-Year financial model

Engineered a single-source model tracking top-tier client analytics, organic/inorganic expansion vectors, comprehensive SG&A structures, and structural corporate overheads.

04 — Automation stack

Comprehensive automated & reporting

Completed PPR packages. Automated and structured critical reporting arrays: pipelines, P&Ls, headcounts, cash flows, ESG compliance, balance sheets, and covenant monitoring trackers.
